The green line starting from St. Thomas Mount Metro converges with Blue Line from Airport at Alandur station.
A train starting from Airport may be either destined to Wimco Nagar Depot Metro or MG Ramachandran Central Metro.
A train starting from St. Thomas Mount is always destined to MG Ramachandran Central Metro.
A train starting from MG Ramachandran Central Metro may be destined to three locations Wimco Nagar Depot Metro or St. Thomas Mount Metro or Chennai Airport.
A train starting from Wimco Nagar Depot Metro is always destined to Chennai Airport.
As per the initial design blue line and green line were separate lines. But the designers looked at the over head of Airport passengers have to change over in Alandur aiport to reach to the bus stations and railway stations. So they converged the lines in Alandur, which gives the possibility for Aiport passengers to reach the bus stations and railways station without having the need for change over in Alandur station, but did not visualize the confusions the passengers will face when taking up their journey in these stations.
The objective of this project is to provide a clear journey path from one metro station to another.
The key inputs from the passenger will be the starting and ending metro stations, based on which it should clearly convey the journey path including the escalators and lifts that they will have to take.
In analysis.md, there are instructions on the difficulties in Alandur station to switch over from one destination to another.
